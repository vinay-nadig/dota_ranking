Welcome to MVIT dota ranking site



Technical :
Each hero is given 3 attributes known as the Cp, Sp, Dm.
Cp = carry potential.
Sp = support potential.
Dm = death margin.

All the 3 attributes are rated on a scale of 0 to 1.
Cp and Sp are pretty self explanatory and based on how a well a hero
can carry and support respectively.
Eg : Naga Siren, Magina, Ursa are all hard carries and have Cp >= 0.8
Rhasta, Lion, Omni Knight are all good supports and have Sp >= 0.7

Death Margin of a hero is a measure of his/her survivability.
Eg : Puck is hard to kill with her "Phase Shift" + "Illusory orb" + an
optional dagger. Hence her death margin is 0.8 (the highest).
Magina has 50% magical damage reduction and a blink. hence his death margin
is 0.7
Venge is usually an initiator and does not scale that well into late game,
hence her dm is 0.3 (one of the lowest)

For a complete list of all heroes and their Cp, Sp and Dm points,
please visit this page.


The average point of a player is calculated based on the hero he plays and the above 3 factors of the hero and
a bonus point is awarded if he is in the winning team.

The actual formulae is :
Avg Points = ( Cp*K + Sp*A - 2*Dm*D + [1] )/Number of matches.
where K = Number of Kills
A = Number of Assists
D = Number of Deaths
[1] = an optional point if the player's team wins.

The whole system is based on the idea that each player should play his hero
to its max potential AND in the proper role.

The system is ofcourse not perfect and needs lots of tweaks and fine tuning.
Any feedback is appreciated.
